Big Spoon Creamery is an artisan ice cream company located in Birmingham, Alabama. We are a small company simply dedicated to carefully sourcing ingredients, thoughtfully crafting the best possible ice cream, and serving our customers and community with purpose. Visit our new storefront in Avondale at the MAKEbhm building, or find us at events throughout the Birmingham area!
